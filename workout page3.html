Simple Diet Recipe

Try this delicious and nutritious recipe:

Avocado and Tomato Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 avocado
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cut the avocado and tomatoes into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Thinly slice the red onion.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  4. Combine the avocado, tomatoes, and red onion in a large bowl.
  5. Pour thedressing over the salad and toss gently.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
